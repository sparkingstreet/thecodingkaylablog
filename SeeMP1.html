My First Website: The Struggle Was Real

9/27/2024

tehcnologies used: Html CSS

Let me tell you, getting my website up and running was no joke. The coding part felt like a breeze comparted to all the behind-the-scenes setup. It tooke me days to figure it out, and there were so many moments when I was ready to throw in the towel. But instead, I pushed through, step by step, and somehow, it all started coming together.

After countless hours(and a few "why am i doing this" moments), I finally connected everything--my GitHub, my repositories, my site--and let me just say, the feeling of seeing it all live? Unreal. I might've actually jumped up and down when it worked.

Now that everything is running smoothly, I'm starting to feel ready for some side work! Who knew setting up my first website would be this big of a roller coaster? There was a point where I seriously considered giving up coding altogehter. Funny how the same thing that almsot made me quit has now become the thing that brings me so much joy--thanks to one little website.
