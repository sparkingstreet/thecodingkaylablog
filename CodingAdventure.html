Welcome Back to the Coding Adventure!

10/07/2024

tehcnologies used: Html CSS JavaScript

Hey there, fellow coders! It’s been a wild ride lately, and I’m here to share a little about my journey. Let’s be real—getting into the nitty-gritty of databases feels like trying to solve a puzzle without all the pieces. While I can whip up HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with ease, diving into database management has been a bit of a struggle. It seems like the simplest tasks often turn into the most challenging ones!

Right now, my comment section is still on the fritz because I’m wrestling with getting my databases live and connected to my website. It’s the same old song and dance around here, but I’m determined to push through.

Yes, I know—I’m still stuck on databases. Trust me, it’s equally frustrating for me. I’d love to talk about something else at this point, but databases have taken over my life like a clingy cat that refuses to leave you alone. I mean, honestly, who knew getting data to actually stay where it’s supposed to would be harder than sleep training a newborn?

As college has kicked back into gear, I find myself juggling my roles as a student, a mom, and an employee. It’s a lot to handle, but I like to think of it as coding in real life: once you find the right code and hit your groove, everything becomes a little smoother. And just like coding, I can only hope that it all gets a bit easier with time.

To give myself a breather from the database dilemma, I’ve decided to step back and start a new project. Sometimes you need to shift your focus and come back with a clear mind!

I’ll keep you updated on my progress. Here’s to finding that perfect code—both in our projects and in life!
